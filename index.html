Building Energy Modeling for Flexible Scheduling in Power Grids

Introduction

Welcome to the official website for the IEEE PES Task Force on Building Energy Modeling for Flexible Scheduling.

This initiative integrates mainstream learning modeling algorithms, visualizes modeling results on both building and city scale, and provides a standardized workflow bridging BIM and power scheduling algorithms.
